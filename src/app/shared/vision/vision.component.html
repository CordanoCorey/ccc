Vision

  • To engage the youth of Carlisle, Pennsylvania in building a better community through partnerships and projects that promote community development.
  • Build the capacity of community/neighborhood-based organizations and work creatively with County and State agencies, so that social and health services are available to all residents, including senior citizens and children.
  • Encourage and assist neighborhood-based community development, particularly along commercial districts, which will create jobs for area residents.
  • To enhance community morale through community beautification projects.
